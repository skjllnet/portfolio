About me

Hello there, my name is Vassil (Vah-cil) and I am a Graphic Designer who fell in love with the idea of creating functional forms. It is true, I like to geek out over code just as much as aesthetics. My favorite languages are JavaScript and ReactJS because the possibilities are nearly endless from web apps to applications and robotics.

Some of my hobbies are riding my bike, or fixing my bike. Working on my car and adding small personal touches to it like new radio heads or interior cabin under-glow lighting. I also play soccer regularly and I hope to travel more frequently and visit different types of library's while I work and learn.
